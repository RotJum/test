Aesculus hippocastanum

Other common names

Conker tree

Origin of the species name

Aesculus is Latin for a type of oak; hippocastanum is Latin for horse chestnut (from horse-shoe-shaped leaf scars).

Family

Sapindaceae
Date planted
August 2009
Lifespan
Horse chestnuts can live for 300 years.
